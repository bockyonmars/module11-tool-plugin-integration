Incident Report: Lab Result Delay

Date: 2026-07-18
Owner: Operations Team
Priority: High

Several patients complained that their lab results were not visible after sample collection. The issue occurred because results were marked as pending even after technician completion. The clinical review step was not clearly explained to patients.

Risks:
- Patient frustration may increase.
- Support tickets may rise.
- Hospitals may lose confidence in the digital record system.

Actions:
- Update patient-facing result status labels.
- Add an explanation for pending, reviewed, and completed results.
- Train support staff to explain the result workflow.
